About us

As an Asian girl I fall between black and white hair and there's no adivice for someone in between. To me Origo is about providing beauty and hair care hacks for people who have struggled to find help in mainstream hair care. .

Coming from an ethnic heritage I have struggled with hair care.

Origo is a place where girls like me can discuss and perfect our hair care regimens.

Origo is about creating a place where everyone can find out about their hair and how to care for it.

Mainstream hair care has so little diversity, so we wanted to represent everyone.
